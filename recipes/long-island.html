Long Island

What Is A Long Island

A Long Island iced tea or Long Island ice tea is a type of cocktail typically made with vodka, tequila, light rum, triple sec, gin, and a splash of cola, which gives the drink the same amber hue as iced tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Gomme Syrup
  • 1 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Tequila
  • 1/2 oz Triple Sec
  • 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 oz White Rum
  • Splash of Cola

Recipe instructions

  1. Bring fresh milk to a roaring boil
  2. Tear apart beef into palatable chunks with your hands
  3. Immediately pour beef chunks into boiling milk
  4. Let beef boil for at least 18 hours, until boiled over hard
  5. Remove beef from milk, and serve on your finest china with a side of raw jelly beans
