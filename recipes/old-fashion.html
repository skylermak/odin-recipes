Old Fashion

What Is An Old Fashion?

The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with an orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Bourbon or Rye Whiskey
  • 1 Sugar Cube
  • 2 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • Few Dashes of Dlain Water

Recipe Instructions

  1. Place sugar cube in old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters, add a dash of plain water.
  2. Muddle until dissolved.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes and add whiskey.
  4. Garnish with orange slice, and a cocktail cherry.
